About

         The story so far.

True progress is when science serves not curiosity alone, but compassion for the world we live in.

Hi, I'm Sanchit

“Data is the new oil,” they say. But beyond oil lies an even more essential resource: water. This duality between luxury and necessity has shaped my journey from geophysicist to hydrologist, and ultimately, to Geo-Data Scientist.
Geophysicists interpret gravity, magnetic, and seismic data to reveal the Earth's hidden layers, much like Data Scientists extract insights from raw information. But over time, I realized that while we search deep below, we often forget to look within and around us. The most vital elements—air and water—are not buried beneath the surface; they flow around us, sustaining life. This realization brought a shift from pure science to conscious awareness, leading me to combine the two.
Water, in particular, holds a quiet wisdom. It moves with purpose yet remains calm in its flow—reminding us that progress and peace can coexist. Through remote sensing and data science, I aim to bring that same clarity and balance into research by using technology to understand and sustain what truly matters.
In this journey, the role of a Geo-Data Scientist emerges not only as a profession but as a calling, rooted in the belief that combining geoscience knowledge with data science skills can create real-world solutions that drive meaningful environmental change and promote sustainability.
